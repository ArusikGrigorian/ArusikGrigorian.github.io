Venus is the Roman goddess of love, beauty,
prosperity, fertility and victory. "Arus"
(from "Arusyak"), is Armenian name of
the planet Venus, which, as you guess, is
named after the Roman goddess. So, "Arus"
and "Venus" are the same phenomenon. Now,
you know the etymology of "Arus" and
can reformulate the main commandment:
